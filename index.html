Welcome to AYOUB COMPUTERS, where our legacy in the tech industry spans over 15 years, marked by a journey of continuous growth and innovation. Established in 2008 as a wholesale and retailer, we have evolved into a dynamic force in the IT distribution landscape.

In the past three years, AYOUB COMPUTERS has experienced rapid development and witnessed the flourishing expansion of our online presence. We are proud to share that our website has become one of the top-ranking platforms in Lebanon, offering an extensive range of tech products and accessories. This accomplishment is a testament to our unwavering commitment to delivering quality and staying at the forefront of the rapidly evolving technology landscape.

At AYOUB COMPUTERS, we pride ourselves on connecting customers to a wide range of well-known brands. By partnering with us, vendors become strategic allies, ready to introduce their high-quality products to the discerning Lebanese market.

As we celebrate our 15-year milestone, AYOUB COMPUTERS remains dedicated to providing our clients with the latest advancements in technology.
